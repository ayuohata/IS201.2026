What's Opanchu Usagi?

Opanchu Usagi is a popular, viral Japanese character created by illustrator Kawaisou ni!. It is a pink, watery-eyed rabbit known for its tragic, unlucky daily life, often wearing white underpants (opantsu).

  1. Why she is loved

    • Often ends up in unlucky situations
    • People can see themselves in it
    • Its sad expressions make it more adorable

  2. Character traits

    • Innocent and kind, even when things go wrong
    • Tries her best but often fails in a clumsy way
    • Emotionally expressive (cries, panics, but keeps going)

  3. Unique appeal / concept

    • Cute, but in a slightly tragic way
    • Humor comes from relatable everyday struggles
    • Simple design but strong emotional storytelling
